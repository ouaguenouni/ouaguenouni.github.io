Efficient Elicitation of Collective Disagreements

Théorie Algorithmique de la Décision et des Jeux (TADJ) · 21 June 2026 · interactive deck

A build-in-the-browser talk: a measure of where a population disagrees (not just whether it does), a hierarchy that grades how much information a disagreement measure really uses, and a low-cognitive-load protocol to elicit it. Built with reveal.js, with live Plotly figures — the skewness × kurtosis moment plane and a population-vs-cognitive-load frontier — that you can drive directly from the slides.
